Workflow

The sales funnel is designed to stay digital from start to draft.

1

Estimate intake

The client submits scope, goals, features, budget band, and timing.

2

Onboarding intake

Assets, inspiration, copy, integrations, and SEO notes are collected asynchronously.

3

Draft generation

Codex and OpenClaw synthesize the brief, generate the repo, and prepare a preview draft.

4

Internal approval

Telegram, Slack, and Gmail route the first pass for approval before the client-facing note is sent.
