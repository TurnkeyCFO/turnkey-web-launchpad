Privacy

Simple privacy terms for people using Turnkey Web.

This page explains what information may be collected when you browse the site, request a quote, or contact Turnkey Web, and how that information is used.

Last updated April 13, 2026 Applies to quote requests and inquiries Questions: ricky@turnkeycfo.com
Privacy in plain language

We only ask for what helps move your project forward.

Contact details, project details, and site usage data are used to respond to inquiries, prepare estimates, and improve the website experience.

What we may collect

When you use the quote builder, onboarding form, or contact links, Turnkey Web may collect your name, email address, phone number, company name, website details, and project information you choose to share.

The site may also collect basic usage information such as page visits, device type, browser information, and interaction data used to understand site performance and improve the experience.

How that information is used

  • To respond to quote requests, audit requests, and project inquiries
  • To prepare pricing guidance, onboarding materials, and follow-up communication
  • To improve site content, performance, and user experience
  • To maintain security, troubleshoot issues, and prevent misuse

Sharing and third-party services

Information may be processed through website hosting, analytics, email, and form-handling tools that support Turnkey Web operations. Information is not sold as a product.

If third-party integrations are used to deliver a project or respond to your inquiry, only the information reasonably needed for that purpose should be shared.

Cookies, analytics, and tracking

Turnkey Web may use analytics or measurement tools to understand traffic, page performance, and conversion behavior. This helps improve page speed, content clarity, and the overall lead experience.

If you prefer not to submit personal information through forms, you can contact Turnkey Web directly by email instead.

Your choices

If you want to update or remove information you have submitted, email ricky@turnkeycfo.com. Requests will be handled as reasonably and practically as possible.
