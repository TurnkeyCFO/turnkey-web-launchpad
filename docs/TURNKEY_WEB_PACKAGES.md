# Turnkey Web Packages

Updated April 13, 2026 for small businesses under $5M in revenue, with pricing tuned for a Texas market position that is competitive but not bargain-bin.

## Market context

- National benchmark sources in 2026 put many web design projects under `$10,000`, with Clutch also reporting a much higher overall average because larger custom engagements pull the mean upward.
- Clutch's current web design pricing guide shows many firms around `$100-$149/hr` for web design, while Texas-based agencies on Clutch often cluster in the `$50-$99/hr` range, with some specialists materially above that.
- WebFX's current 2026 pricing pages show website, SEO, PPC, and maintenance ranges that are useful as an upper-mid market benchmark.

## Recommended launch offers

### 1. Landing Page Sprint

- **Turnkey Web recommended price:** `$1,500-$2,500`
- **Typical market range:** roughly `$1,000-$3,500`
- **Best for:** one offer, paid traffic, event pages, lead magnets, service-specific launches
- **Include:**
  - strategy call optional, not required
  - one conversion-focused page
  - mobile optimization
  - copy polish using client inputs
  - contact or quote form
  - analytics/tracking setup
  - basic on-page SEO

### 2. Business Website Build

- **Turnkey Web recommended price:** `$3,500-$6,500`
- **Typical market range:** roughly `$3,000-$10,000` for small service businesses
- **Best for:** businesses needing a modern 3-8 page trust-building website
- **Include:**
  - discovery via intake, not mandatory meetings
  - custom homepage + internal page system
  - service pages
  - trust/proof sections
  - lead capture forms
  - mobile responsiveness
  - analytics + search console setup
  - launch support

### 3. Website Refresh

- **Turnkey Web recommended price:** `$2,000-$4,500`
- **Typical market range:** roughly `$1,500-$6,000`
- **Best for:** existing sites that need visual cleanup, stronger messaging, and higher conversion without a full rebuild
- **Include:**
  - page-by-page refresh plan
  - homepage redesign
  - conversion improvements
  - performance cleanup
  - selective copy rewrites
  - analytics and CTA cleanup

### 4. Website Care Plan

- **Turnkey Web recommended price:** `$250-$900/mo`
- **Typical market range:** roughly `$100-$1,500+/mo`, depending on whether the provider is only maintaining or actively improving
- **Best for:** post-launch support, small edits, reporting, ongoing improvements
- **Include by tier:**
  - `Care Lite` `$250/mo`: updates, backups, plugin/theme maintenance, uptime checks, 30 minutes of edits
  - `Care Growth` `$450/mo`: everything above plus 2 hours of edits, monthly report, Core Web Vitals review
  - `Care Priority` `$900/mo`: everything above plus 5 hours of edits, CRO suggestions, priority queue

## Add-ons

### SEO Foundations

- **Recommended price:** `$500-$1,500` one-time
- **Typical market range:** roughly `$500-$2,500`
- **Use when:** the client needs metadata, local targeting, search console setup, sitemap cleanup, and page-level SEO basics before a retainer

### Local SEO Retainer

- **Recommended price:** `$750-$1,500/mo`
- **Typical market range:** roughly `$500-$2,500+/mo`
- **Use when:** the client needs map pack and service-area visibility but is still a smaller local business

### Monthly SEO Growth

- **Recommended price:** `$1,500-$3,000/mo`
- **Typical market range:** often starts around `$1,500-$2,500/mo` and climbs fast with competitiveness
- **Use when:** the client wants content, technical work, page expansion, and sustained search growth

### Google Ads Setup

- **Recommended price:** `$750-$1,500` one-time
- **Typical market range:** roughly `$750-$2,500`
- **Use when:** the client wants a properly structured starter account without committing to full management yet

### Google Ads Management

- **Recommended price:** `$500-$1,250/mo` for lower-spend SMB accounts
- **Typical market range:** often `$650-$975/mo` at the low-to-mid end or `12%-15%` of spend at larger budgets
- **Use when:** the client is spending enough on ads to justify weekly optimization

## Suggested packaging strategy

- Lead with only four launch offers on the site:
  - `Landing Page Sprint`
  - `Business Website Build`
  - `Website Refresh`
  - `Website Care Plan`
- Keep SEO and ads as add-ons or phase-two expansion offers.
- For Texas businesses under `$5M`, position Turnkey Web in the **strong-value middle**:
  - above DIY/freelancer commodity pricing
  - below premium agency retainers
  - justified by speed, async execution, and clearer systems

## Recommended pricing language

- Use **"starting at"** language on the public site.
- Show **range-based estimates** in the intake flow.
- Keep final quotes anchored to:
  - page count
  - feature complexity
  - timeline urgency
  - asset readiness
  - whether SEO/ads setup is bundled
