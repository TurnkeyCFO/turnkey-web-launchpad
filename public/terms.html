Terms

Terms for using the Turnkey Web website and quote tools.

These terms explain the general rules for browsing the site, using the pricing estimator, and reviewing the example work shown throughout the site.

Last updated April 13, 2026 Estimate ranges are planning guidance Contact: ricky@turnkeycfo.com
Important note

The estimator is a planning tool, not a final contract.

Final pricing, timeline, and scope are confirmed after project details are reviewed and the work is formally approved.

Website use

You may browse the site, review example work, and use the quote builder for lawful business purposes. Content should not be copied, republished, or used in a misleading way without permission.

Pricing and estimate guidance

Published pricing and estimator ranges are intended to help prospective clients understand likely starting points. They are not a binding quote, final proposal, or guarantee of exact project cost.

Final scope and pricing depend on the project details, required integrations, revision needs, timeline, and any additional work requested after the initial estimate.

Example work and reference pages

The site may link to concept pages, portfolio examples, or reference builds. Those examples are shown to illustrate style, structure, and capability, and should not be treated as a guarantee that any specific result will occur for every project.

Project decisions and approvals

Work begins only after the project details, pricing, and any required approvals are confirmed. Launch timing, SEO outcomes, lead volume, and conversion performance depend on many factors outside the site itself, including offer quality, traffic source, competition, and follow-up process.
